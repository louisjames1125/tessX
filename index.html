About Tess

My name is Tessa Sanders and am a barber located in Kokomo, Indiana. I started my career by studying cosmetology, hair styling, and esthetics at Summit Salon Academy. While I was attending school I learned how to cut a wide variety of hair for both men and women. Upon graduation I joined Barlows Barbshop and have been an employee for their team ever since.

In my spare time I love to take photographs of whatever catches my eye. Click the button below if you want to see some of my photography work.
